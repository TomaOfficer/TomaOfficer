Overview

FoundationLab is a software design agency in Boston, MA. I helped them build this dashboard for reviewing client leads and estimating a project's profitability. FoundationLab's primary goal was to make something they could use themselves; their secondary goal was to sell it as a SaaS product. I was responsible for information architecture, wireframes, and visual design.
