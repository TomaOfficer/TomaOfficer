About Thomas

I'm a UX designer with an interest in marketing, visual design, and front-end development. I like the title Product Designer because I'm proud to be a generalist. My name is pronounced "Toma." I was born in France, raised in New Hampshire, and went to law school in Scotland.

Professional Background

I have a year of product design experience: 3 months as a freelancer, 9 months working on ThroughCompany. Before that, I was a due diligence analyst at CrowdCheck where I helped small companies prepare for online investment campaigns. It was during that time that I cofounded ThroughCompany, a SaaS product to address the corporate governance issues I saw everyday as an analyst. Building ThroughCompany is how I first learned product design. I also recently helped run the Boston Unreasonable Lab, an accelerator program for social entrepreneurs.
