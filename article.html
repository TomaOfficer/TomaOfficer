In my experience, working on design in a small tech team requires you to juggle two primary concerns: (1) What does the market desire?; and (2) Is our product usable? Though I've never worked in a large company, I can imagine they do it differently than the tiny organizations I've accustomed to; I can imagine they divide these two concerns into different design departments, each with their own teams and goals. This makes sense. Desirability and usability are two very different concerns. This explains I handle each.
