Project Summary

DISCO is a web and mobile app I built while enrolled in the Bloc UX/UI program. It’s a tool for building itineraries and it’s helpful for traveling or changing your routine.

The Challenge

Simplifying an Idea

DISCO started out as a hackathon project. In October 2015, I went to New Orleans to participate in a 54-hour hackathon called Startup Weekend. I joined a team that said it was going to work on the issue of traveling and finding things to do. This appealed to me because it seemed, at its core, to be an issue about sincerity and engagement.
