Overview

Echo is a note-taking tool for online writers to capture and develop their ideas. My cofounder and I pivoted into Echo after 5 months of tinkering with AI. We grew the team, shipped a multi-platform product, and built an audience of AI curious writers.

Shipped a multi-platform product across 6 platforms in under a year.

landing page 2025

Goals

1
Capture ideas at the moment of inspiration. Writers have ideas everywhere. Echo needed to be there when inspiration struck, whether on a walk, in the shower, or at the desk.
2
Transform voice notes into written drafts. Most voice note apps let recordings pile up. Echo uses AI to transform raw voice memos into structured, editable text.
3
Meet writers where they are. Different writers work in different environments. Echo needed to be multi-platform from the start.

Capture

The core of Echo is frictionless voice capture. One tap to record, automatic transcription, and AI-powered organization. Writers can capture a thought in seconds without breaking their flow.

voice capture

Develop

Raw voice notes become structured drafts through AI assistance. Echo doesn't just transcribe - it helps writers expand, organize, and refine their ideas into publishable content.

AI-assisted development

Write

The final step is turning developed ideas into finished pieces. Echo provides a distraction-free writing environment where writers can polish and export their work.

writing environment

Multi-platform

To meet users where they are, at the moment of inspiration, Echo had to be multi-platform. Echo can be found on iOS, Apple Watch, Mac, MCP, Web app, and Chrome Extension.
