Services

Usability Audit

I evaluate how real users experience your product and pinpoint the friction that slows them down. You get clear, actionable recommendations that make your core flows feel faster, simpler, and more obvious.

What I focus on: where people get stuck, what they misunderstand, and how to make your product feel effortless.

User Feedback Sprint

I talk directly with your customers so you don't have to guess. A short, structured sprint that surfaces the insights your team has been missing — how users think, where they struggle, and what actually matters to them.

The outcome: crisp, founder-friendly guidance rooted in real conversations.

Activation & Flywheel Design

I map how users discover value in your product and turn those moments into growth loops. This is where activation, delight, and natural sharing meet — all designed with a light touch and a clear narrative.

The goal: help your product create its own momentum.
