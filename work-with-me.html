Usability Audit

I evaluate how real users experience your product and pinpoint the friction that slows them down. You get clear, actionable recommendations that make your core flows feel faster, simpler, and more obvious.

  • Friction Map

    A visual breakdown of where users get stuck, what confuses them, and the specific moments that cause drop-offs in your core flows.

  • Actionable Recommendations

    Prioritized fixes with clear explanations of what to change, why it matters, and how it will improve the user experience.

  • Usability Report

    A concise document summarizing findings, impact, and next steps that your team can reference and act on immediately.

User Feedback Sprint

I talk directly with your customers so you don't have to guess. A short, structured sprint that surfaces the insights your team has been missing — how users think, where they struggle, and what actually matters to them.

  • User Interview Summary

    Key insights from direct conversations with your customers, organized by themes and patterns that reveal how they actually use your product.

  • Prioritized Insights

    A ranked list of findings that matter most, with clear explanations of what users value and what's blocking their success.

Activation & Flywheel Design

I help teams map the full journey from first touch to true activation — not just where users click, but where they feel the "Aha!" that makes your product indispensable. Together, we define what an activated user really looks like, identify the moments that create that shift, and redesign the experience so more people reliably reach it. From there, I work with you to "close the loop": turning that Aha! moment into natural momentum where each activated user attracts the next one.

  • Activation Map

    Map user's experience from first touch to Aha! moment.

  • Aha! Narrative & Redesign Concepts

    A concise explanation of your true Aha! moment with lightweight concepts showing how to guide more users there, faster.

  • Flywheel Blueprint

    A simple, visual loop showing how activation leads naturally into sharing or inviting, turning each activated user into the next source of growth.

Information Architecture Reset

I help you revisit your product's navigation structure and underlying mental models. Especially useful after years of rapid shipping where the product now feels heavier, harder to navigate, and harder to evolve.

  • Information Architecture Map

    A reorganized structure of your product’s pages, features, flows, and concepts that actually matches how your users think.

  • Navigation & Naming System

    Clear, consistent naming and labeling that reduces mental load and makes the product intuitive at a glance.
