Services

Usability Audit

I evaluate how real users experience your product and pinpoint the friction that slows them down. You get clear, actionable recommendations that make your core flows feel faster, simpler, and more obvious.

  • Friction Map — A visual breakdown of where users get stuck, what confuses them, and the specific moments that cause drop-offs in your core flows.
  • Actionable Recommendations — Prioritized fixes with clear explanations of what to change, why it matters, and how it will improve the user experience.
  • Usability Report — A concise document summarizing findings, impact, and next steps that your team can reference and act on immediately.

What I focus on: where people get stuck, what they misunderstand, and how to make your product feel effortless.

User Feedback Sprint

I talk directly with your customers so you don't have to guess. A short, structured sprint that surfaces the insights your team has been missing — how users think, where they struggle, and what actually matters to them.

  • User Interview Summary — Key insights from direct conversations with your customers, organized by themes and patterns that reveal how they actually use your product.
  • Prioritized Insights — A ranked list of findings that matter most, with clear explanations of what users value and what's blocking their success.
  • Action Plan — Concrete next steps based on user feedback, with recommendations for what to build, change, or investigate further.

The outcome: crisp, founder-friendly guidance rooted in real conversations.

Activation & Flywheel Design

I help teams map the full journey from first touch to true activation — not just where users click, but where they feel the "Aha!" that makes your product indispensable. Together, we define what an activated user really looks like, identify the moments that create that shift, and redesign the experience so more people reliably reach it. From there, I work with you to "close the loop": turning that Aha! moment into natural momentum where each activated user attracts the next one.

  • Activation Map — A clear journey from first touch → "Aha!" → activation, highlighting friction, drop-offs, and the moments that actually change user behavior.
  • Aha! Narrative & Redesign Concepts — A concise explanation of your true Aha! moment with lightweight concepts showing how to guide more users there, faster.
  • Flywheel Blueprint — A simple, visual loop showing how activation leads naturally into sharing or inviting, turning each activated user into the next source of growth.

The goal: help your product create its own momentum.
