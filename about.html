An Introduction

I'm Thomas ("Toma") Officer. I was born in France, raised in New Hampshire, and went to law school in Scotland. I am an entrepreneur, designer, and a lawyer.

Professional Background

  • 2012 passed the New York bar. I was interested in investment crowdfunding. This brought me to CrowdCheck where I was a due diligence analyst for the next 2.5 years. I'm still interested in the idea of pooling my money with like-minded people to nurture companies we believe should succeed.
  • 2014 cofounded ThroughCompany, a SaaS product for startup corporate governance. Building ThroughCompany is how I first learned product design and front-end development.
  • 2015 helped launch Unreasonable Labs Boston, a startup accelerator for social entrepreneurs.
