About Thomas

I'm a UX designer with an interest in marketing, visual design, and front-end development. I like the title Product Designer because I'm proud to be a generalist. My name is pronounced "Toma." I was born in France, raised in New Hampshire, and went to law school in Scotland.

Current Projects

I spend a lot of my time on side projects as I wrap up my UX/UI Design apprenticeship at Bloc. One of these projects is Startup Review, a facilitated discussion board for critiquing and improving startup strategy (coming soon). I also do freelance design work at FoundationLab, a software design agency in Boston, MA.

Professional Background

I have a year of experience in product design: 3 months as a freelancer, 9 months as an entrepreneur working on my own company. Before that, I was a due diligence analyst at CrowdCheck for 2.5 years where I helped small companies prepare for online investment campaigns. It was during that time that I cofounded ThrougCompany, a SaaS product to address the corporate governance issues I saw everyday as an analyst. Building ThroughCompany is how I first learned product design.
