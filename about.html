An Introduction

I'm Thomas ("Toma") Officer. I was born in France, raised in New Hampshire, and went to law school in Scotland and Vermont. I am an entrepreneur, designer, and a lawyer.

I believe authenticity is the primary virtue in the face of an absurd existence, and this is an absurd existence. This line of thought is probably the most French thing about me.

I've worked hard to cultivate a beginner's mind, so if you have a crazy idea please don't hesitate to share it.

Professional Background

I graduated law school in 2012. I was a research analyst for a small due diligence firm for 2.5 years. I started designing products in 2014 when I cofounded a company. Most recently, I helped launch a startup accelerator for social entrepreneurs: Unreasonable Labs Boston.
