CV and Bio

welcome, i'm thomas. A product designer who works across marketing, design, and development.

Up until very recently, I was a cofounder and designer at Echo, a voice note and AI-assisted writing app that helps online writers capture ideas and develop them into content. Before that, I cofounded Afterpattern, a no-code app builder for lawyers (acquired in 2021).

I've spent 10 years in startups teaching myself whatever was needed; from design to development to GTM. If something needs to be done, I figure it out.

I was born in France, raised in NH, and went to law school in Scotland. I love the woods, walkable cities, and long dinners with friends. Quality conversation is a core value. It feels good to be on the same page, and even better to feel empowered to act on it. I'm thankful for everyone who's allowed me the opportunity to grok, grow, and build together.
