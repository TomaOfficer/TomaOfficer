Welcome ― I'm Thomas.

I'm a French American that loves coffeeshops, scotch, good dialogue, techno, and the Terra Ignota novel series.

For the past 7 years, I've been working in legal tech for a series of small and very small companies. I currently work at Community.lawyer, a company I cofounded in 2016. Our goal is to make legal service more accessible to everyday people.

I'm a self-taught designer and front-end web developer. My work style prioritizes speed to launch. I believe you can't design in a vacuum; until someone uses your product, everything is a hypothesis.

I was born in France, raised in New Hampshire, and went to law school in Scotland. I currently live in Brooklyn.
