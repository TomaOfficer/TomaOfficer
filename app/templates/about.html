Seasoned tech entrepreneur with deep expertise in product design and frontend development, and a growing expertise in full-stack web development (focus in Python).

My career began after law school. I was the 7th hire at a tech company that only hired lawyers (the product was a quasi legal service so it was important for the company to be able to flip a switch and become a law firm in a moment's notice). This is where I was introduced to software and the business of software development. I discovered product design and knew, immediately, that this was the skill I wanted to be able to provide as a cofounder of my own software business.

By 2016, 4 years out of law school, I'm accepted as a product design fellow at the Blue Ridge Labs @ Robin Hood incubator program. What follows is a 5 year journey of finding cofounders, launching a product, pivoting, digging for product market fit, finding it, and then selling the company. I served the team as our only designer, primary frontend developer, sales lead, branding, marketing, and customer support.

After the acquisition, for the next year and a half, I experienced working in a formal design team of a large tech company. This is where I am in late 2022 when ChatGPT comes out. I remember this feeling of tinkering with a new tool and the lightening bolt realization that this something I want to move towards.

Today, I'm a novice yet scrappy 1-person development team. I'm an efficient no-code developer, and clever product designer. I'm looking for the next mission to devote myself to.
