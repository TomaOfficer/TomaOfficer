JP Porchfest

UX + UI, mobile app

JP Porchfest is a neighborhood-wide concert that operates like an open studio. Artists perform on porches across Boston's Jamainca Plain neighborhood, for free.

This project is an effort to build a mobile app to make it easier for participants to choose their own adventure of porches to visit from the schedule of over 125 performances.
