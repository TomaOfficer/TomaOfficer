Speak, friend, and enter. Welcome to my corner of the internet. I'm Thomas, a product designer working remotely from Lebanon, NH. I've been working on one startup or another since graduating law school in 2012.

I'm currently working on Echo, an AI notebook for developing ideas into clear thinking and writing. I spend my workdays talking to customers, designing in Figma, coding in Cursor, and building an audience on social media.

Previously, I cofounded Afterpattern, a no-code app builder for lawyers (acquired 2021). Afterpattern grew out of Community.lawyer, a referral management platform for bar associations and legal aid. The idea for a better referral management platform came out of my time as a Design Fellow at the Blue Ridge Labs social impact tech incubator in Brooklyn (2016).
