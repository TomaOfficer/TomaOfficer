Design Education

Self-directed.

Learning while doing is effective but lonely. My biggest issue isn't finding resources to engage with (e.g. books, articles, online courses), but rather finding people to engage with. An important part of a design education is the critique. Learning how to explain or defend your design choices is an essential skill.

Here are some books that I've found particularly helpful:

  • UX Strategy by Jamie Levy.
  • Value Proposition Design by Strategyzer.
  • Change by Design by Tim Brown.
