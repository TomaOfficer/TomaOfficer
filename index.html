I'm Thomas (“Toma” ) Officer, a product designer and lawyer in Brooklyn, NY.

Currently

I'm a Design Fellow at Blue Ridge Labs @ Robin Hood, a Brooklyn-based incubator program. Our mandate is to create a legaltech product for social impact. The incubator runs from June - October, 2016.

Previously

  1. I was a Product Designer at FoundationLab, a strategy/design/development firm based out of Boston, MA.
  2. I was a Research Analyst at CrowdCheck, a due diligence and disclosure platform for investment crowdfunding portals. With less jargon: I helped companies organize the disclosures required for an online investment campaign.
  3. I graduated from Vermont Law School with an LLM in Amercian Legal Studies. This complimented my LLB from the University of Edinburgh and allowed me to join the New York Bar Association.

Project briefs

Forward Gifts

Freelance project. Website that connects Boston vendors with new customers by facilitating gift giving among new users. I helped over the course of several months on usability research, branding, and visual design.

Freelance project. Online word processor specifically for writing journal entries. I made the UI design and branding choices seen here.
